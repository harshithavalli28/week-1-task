Admission Process

Admissions to the Institute are made along with the other Engineering/Pharmacy colleges in the state through a common entrance test (TGEAPCET) conducted by the Govt. of Telangana State/TSCHE.


B.Tech - CSE, CSE(AI & ML), CSE(Data Science), ECE & CIVIL:


The admission pattern to B. Tech is as follows: The minimum qualification for admission to the first year of the B. Tech course is a pass in the Intermediate (10 + 2) conducted by the board of Intermediate Education, Govt. of Telangana State/A.P or any other examination recognized as equivalent thereto with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as optional subjects. 70 % of the seats are allotted based on the merit in the TGEAPCET. 30 % of the seats are earmarked for Management/NRI candidates.


B. Tech Lateral Entry (CSE, CSE(AI & ML), CSE(Data Science), IT, ECE & CIVIL):


The admission pattern to B. Tech Lateral Entry is as follows: The minimum qualification for admission to the Second year of the B. Tech course is a pass in Diploma/B.Sc(Mathematics) holders, are admitted in the second year of B. Tech .to the extent of 10% of intake based on the merit in the TGECET, under lateral entry scheme.


B. Pharmacy:


The admission pattern to B. Pharmacy is as follows: The minimum qualification for admission to the first year of the B. Pharmacy course is a pass in the Intermediate (10 + 2) conducted by the board of Intermediate Education, Govt. of Telangana State/A.P or any other examination recognized as equivalent thereto with Mathematics, Biology, Physics, and Chemistry as optional subjects. 70 % of the seats are allotted based on the merit in the TGEAPCETB. 30 % of the seats are earmarked for Management/NRI candidates.


MBA:


The admission pattern to MBA courses are as follows: The minimum qualification for admission to the first year of the MBA is a pass in the undergraduate course (10 + 2 + 3). 70 % of the seats are allotted based on the merit in the TGICET. 30 % of the seats are earmarked for Management/NRI candidates.
